About us

Ivolunteer is a non-profit organization devoted to rescue, protect, rehabilitate and release African elephants back into their natural habitats. Since there are many examples of animal exploitation within the tourist industry, for example, photo animals, animals performing in degrading shows, and elephant camps not to mention a thriving illegal trade in wild animals for pets and medicine. Vital part of our mission is to educate and encourage local people, tourists and the international community to appreciate, understand and protect wildlife and their natural habitats.

Rescue

There are no natural predators to a grown-up elephant. Their size and strength are effective enough against big cats and other hunters.Unfortunately, elephants have a lot that humans can make a profit with,(ivory trade, riding abilities etc). IVolunteer org. work around the clock to rescue captive elephants and defend them against poachers/traffickers.

Rehabilitate

We make sure to rehabilitate the rescued elephants as far as is feasible, before releasing them back into the wild repopulating forest areas in which they are already endangered or extinct. Giving them the best opportunity to live the rest of their lives free and in a safe environment, as close to nature as possible and providing them with the best care possible.

Release

With our soft release project, we are running a final test to ensure the elephant has healed and is ready to return to the wild. Re-introduction are most times very difficult with elephants that have been traumatised or were very young when captured.
